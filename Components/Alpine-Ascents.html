Elevating the Human Spirit: About Alpine Ascents

Our Story

What began in 1986 as a small group of passionate climbers has evolved into a global leader in mountaineering and wilderness education. Alpine Ascents was founded on a simple but powerful belief: that the mountains offer a unique stage for personal growth, resilience, and unparalleled discovery.

Based in the Pacific Northwest, we have spent decades bridging the gap between aspiring trekkers and the world's most formidable peaks.

The AAI Philosophy

Safety Through Education: We build informed climbers through training and experience.

Elite Leadership: World-class guides ensure safety and excellence.

Environmental Stewardship: We follow Leave No Trace principles.

Why We Climb

Climbing is not just about reaching the summit — it's about growth, discipline, and perspective.
